This is the 1990 Elektra DMM pressing of Kill ’Em All — one of the most sought-after non-’83 variants due to its Direct Metal Mastering, which delivers sharper transients, tighter bass response, and a louder, punchier overall sound compared to earlier mastering versions. Even better, this copy retains the original shrinkwrap and hype sticker, making it significantly rarer for collectors. The grey Elektra labels used in this period were short-lived, adding to its desirability. For Metallica collectors building full pressing variants, the 1990 DMM grey-label edition is a key piece.
